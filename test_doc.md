# Project Omega Overview

**Project Omega** is a top-secret initiative lead by **Dr. Sarah Connor**.
The primary goal is to develop an **Autonomous Cyber Defense System** (ACDS).

## Key Metadata

- **Budget**: $50,000,000
- **Timeline**: Q4 2025
- **Location**: Cyberdyne Systems HQ, California

## Risk Factors

We have identified potential conflicts with **Skynet protocols**.
The **T-800 unit** has shown promising results in initial trials.
